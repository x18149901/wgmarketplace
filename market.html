Welcome to the maretplace! As you an see there's a handy filter on the left hand side of the page, use this to streamline the number of listings you see to suit your needs. Now, we like to protect both our customers and our sellers, so we have a couple of rules that you have to follow. The number 1 rule is TREAT EACHOTHER WITH RESPECT, this is a friendly community. For the renters; first you must pay the deposit and rent total upfront, which will be held in eskrow, this is non-negotiable, secondly, you must wash with a laundry service and provide proof, third, return the rented items in the condition that you received them. Sellers; firstly, no scamming (obviously), second, photos must be provided and timestamped just before sending clothes as proof of condition, thirdly, no "private" clothing/accessories may be sold here (e.g. underwear), there's other websites for that kind of thing, finally, you cannot sell items as a method of sending another, e.g. if you sell a tshirt but actually use it to ship a tshirt and another, non fashion item, is not allowed.
