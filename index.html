Hi! Welcome to W&G's Clothes Market! Here you can rent, lend, buy and sell clothes to others! We set up W&G's Clothes Market because we belive in making life more affordable, cutting down on waste and letting people try before they buy. In this online community you can rent out, buy and lend clothing, maybe you need to go to a once off event or maybe you just think you'd love a certain look but don't want to commit until you've tried it out! Well we've got you covered for all your clothing and fashion needs! If you want to get going straight away, you can click on the market link in the nav bar above or go straight to what you're interested in and click one of the images below to bring you straight to buy you want! We need to look out for our vendors, so if you want to rent any items of clothing or sell any clothes, please sign up or log in if you're already a member.
